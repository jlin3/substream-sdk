Generic game highlight criteria (for games that don't fit specific genres):

HIGH VALUE (80-100):
- Major achievements or milestones
- Boss defeats or difficult challenge completions
- Rare item drops or discoveries
- Dramatic story moments with strong emotional impact
- Impressive mechanical skill demonstrations
- Unexpected or humorous events
- Close calls with dramatic escapes

MEDIUM VALUE (50-79):
- Satisfying gameplay sequences
- Puzzle solutions or clever strategies
- Exploration discoveries (new areas, hidden secrets)
- Character progression moments (level ups, unlocks)
- Good combat sequences with varied action

LOW VALUE (20-49):
- Routine gameplay loops
- Inventory management
- Standard navigation between areas
- Dialogue sequences (unless dramatic)

IGNORE (0-19):
- Loading screens and menus
- Settings adjustment
- Idle moments
- Repetitive grinding without variety

Audio cues: achievement sounds, dramatic music shifts, character voice lines with emotional emphasis, environmental audio spikes (explosions, crashes), player reactions.
