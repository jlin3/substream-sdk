Battle Royale (Fortnite, Apex, Warzone, PUBG, etc.) highlight criteria:

HIGH VALUE (80-100):
- Victory Royale / Champion squad moment
- Final circle clutch plays
- Squad wipes (eliminating full enemy team)
- Insane sniper shots or long-range eliminations
- Creative building plays (Fortnite) or movement tech (Apex)
- Winning gunfights while extremely low HP
- Hot drop success (multiple early kills in contested areas)

MEDIUM VALUE (50-79):
- Clean 1v1 gunfight wins
- Successful third-party plays
- Good rotations under fire
- Vehicle plays (explosions, dramatic chases)
- Reviving teammates under pressure
- Finding legendary/mythic loot (brief moment)

LOW VALUE (20-49):
- Standard looting
- Running across open terrain
- Single eliminations on unaware players
- Routine circle rotations

IGNORE (0-19):
- Inventory management
- Long looting sequences
- Waiting in bushes/buildings with no action
- Loading/lobby screens
- Dropping from the bus/plane (unless immediately into action)

Audio cues: elimination sounds, shield break/crack sounds, storm/circle warnings, victory music, announcer callouts ("You are the champion"), gunfire intensity spikes.
