Sports game (FIFA, NBA 2K, Madden, Rocket League, etc.) highlight criteria:

HIGH VALUE (80-100):
- Goals / Touchdowns / Baskets in clutch moments (final minutes, overtime)
- Game-winning plays
- Incredible skill moves or fakes
- Long-range shots or half-court buzzer beaters
- Aerial goals in Rocket League
- Perfect team plays (passing sequences, fast breaks)
- Comeback moments

MEDIUM VALUE (50-79):
- Standard goals/scores with good execution
- Good defensive plays (saves, blocks, interceptions)
- Skillful dribbling or ball control
- Breakaway plays
- Near-misses that hit the post/bar (dramatic)

LOW VALUE (20-49):
- Routine passes
- Standard defensive positioning
- Free kicks/penalties without drama
- Substitutions or tactical changes

IGNORE (0-19):
- Menu navigation
- Team management screens
- Replays of already-captured moments (avoid duplication)
- Cutscenes between plays
- Half-time screens

Audio cues: crowd cheering spikes, commentator excitement, goal horns/celebrations, whistle sounds, music changes on important plays.
