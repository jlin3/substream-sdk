MOBA (League of Legends, Dota 2, etc.) highlight criteria:

HIGH VALUE (80-100):
- Team fight wins with multiple kills
- Pentakills / Rampages / Ultra kills
- Baron/Roshan steals
- Game-winning pushes or base races
- Outplay mechanics (1v2+, tower dives that work, jukes)
- Perfect ultimate ability combos

MEDIUM VALUE (50-79):
- Solo kills in lane with outplay
- Successful ganks with good execution
- Dragon/objective secures
- Clutch escapes at low HP
- Tower dives that succeed

LOW VALUE (20-49):
- Standard last-hitting / farming
- Routine rotations
- Single assists without significant contribution
- Deaths without counterplay

IGNORE (0-19):
- Shopping at fountain
- Walking between lanes
- Warding (unless it leads to a play)
- Early-game passive farming

Audio cues: announcer voice lines ("Double Kill!", "Pentakill!", "Legendary!"), ability sound effects, ping sounds during fights, team communication intensity.
