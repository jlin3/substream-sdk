FPS / Shooter game highlight criteria:

HIGH VALUE (80-100):
- Multi-kills (double, triple, quad, ace/team wipe)
- Clutch rounds (1vN situations won)
- Headshot streaks or sniper highlights
- Defuse/plant under pressure
- Game-winning kills or round-winning plays
- "Impossible" shots (no-scopes, wallbangs, cross-map)

MEDIUM VALUE (50-79):
- Clean single kills with good mechanics
- Successful flanks or strategic plays
- Ability/ultimate usage with multiple kills
- Narrow escapes that lead to a kill
- Good team coordination moments

LOW VALUE (20-49):
- Standard gunfights with nothing special
- Kills on unaware/AFK opponents
- Deaths (unless dramatically funny or part of a clutch attempt)

IGNORE (0-19):
- Walking/running without action
- Buying/inventory management
- Spectating other players
- Loading screens or menus

Audio cues to watch for: kill feed sounds, multi-kill announcements, "ace" callouts, bomb plant/defuse sounds, crowd cheering (in esports context).
